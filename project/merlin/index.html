Merlin

Product Manager•The Artificer's AI

A realtime voice agent that answers inbound prospects in the browser, qualifies them, and either books a discovery briefing or captures a structured requirements brief.

Situation

Discovery meetings were being spent on calls that could have been qualified up front. The intake brief puts the goal plainly: let a caller describe what they want built without sitting through a meeting.

Task

  • Ship a voice agent that handles an inbound prospect end to end — greet, qualify, explain the offering, book a 45-minute briefing.
  • Add a second mode that interviews a caller and turns the conversation into a structured requirements brief the team can act on.

Result

Live at merlin.artificersai.com and embedded on the studio's intake page, with a push-button deploy that health-checks the running commit and can roll back. Both modes run off one binary.

My Contribution

  • Decided the model gets treated as untrusted. It can call tools, but it does not get the final say on whether the work actually happened.
  • A live call once ended by announcing the brief had been submitted when no brief existed — no file, no email, nothing. The recap gate lived inside the submit tool, so skipping the tool skipped the gate. Moved the gate up into the dispatcher: the agent cannot end a call claiming success unless the submit actually succeeded.
  • Required booking tools to prove they know what time it is — any of the five booking calls is rejected unless the clock was checked in the last 60 seconds, because a confidently wrong booking time is worse than a retry.
  • Decoupled the caller identity form from the model after live calls showed it looping on the greeting and calling the tool late or never. The form now paints on a timer regardless, with a watchdog that reprompts and eventually cuts the call.
  • Suppressed caller transcripts until the form is submitted, because the model emits phantom transcriptions from mic noise the moment the socket opens.

Key Results

20 Tools the agent can call mid-conversation

Filtered per variant — the sales agent sees 18, the intake interviewer sees 8, from one registry on one deployed binary.

Two variants, one binary Sales funnel and intake interview

Same process and audio pipeline; the URL decides which prompt and which slice of the tool registry the caller gets.

Full booking lifecycle Find, book, reschedule, cancel

Including recovery when a slot is taken between offer and confirmation — it re-fetches alternatives rather than failing the call.

Under 900ms Latency budget, speech-stop to audio

The design target the audio pipeline was built around — raw PCM frames forwarded the moment they arrive, never buffering a full response.
