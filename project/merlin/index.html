A realtime voice agent that qualifies inbound prospects in the browser, then books the briefing or captures the brief.

Situation

Every inbound enquiry cost a discovery meeting before anyone knew whether it was worth having. The studio was spending its scarcest asset — senior time — qualifying strangers.

Task

  • Qualify inbound prospects without putting a person on the call.
  • Send the good ones to a booked briefing; turn the rest into a written brief.

Result

Prospects qualify themselves in conversation and leave with a briefing booked or a structured brief in the team's inbox. Senior time now goes to calls that are already worth having.

My Contribution

  • Framed the problem as qualification, not automation. The goal was never to replace discovery — it was to stop paying for discovery that shouldn't happen.
  • Split one product into two front doors once it was clear a cold prospect and a client with a spec want completely different conversations.
  • Ruled that the agent doesn't get the last word on whether work happened, after a live call ended announcing a brief that was never submitted.
  • Set the standard that a wrong booking costs more trust than a retry costs patience — so booking tools must prove they know the time before they can act.
